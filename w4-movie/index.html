HOME

Chris, a senior in high school, has plans to attend a romantic anniversary dinner with her boyfriend. When he cancels last minute, she decides to take a babysitting job. Oh, what an adventure this would turn out to be. From crushes to runaways, car troubles, money troubles, car theft rings, stage appearances, skyscraper adventures and one misfortune after the next; this crew sticks together and winds up having the night of their life!
